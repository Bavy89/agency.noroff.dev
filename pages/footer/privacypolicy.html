Privacy Policy for Noroff Jobs Agency

Introduction

Welcome to Noroff Jobs Agency, the premier platform for professional developers seeking employment opportunities and companies in search of qualified candidates. This privacy policy outlines how we handle and protect the personal information of our users.

Information Collection

We collect personal information such as names, email addresses, professional qualifications, employment history, and other relevant details from developers. For companies, we collect contact information and details regarding their recruitment needs. This information is gathered through our website forms, user profiles, and interactions with our services.

Use of Information

The information collected is primarily used to match developers with suitable job opportunities and to assist companies in finding the right candidates. We may also use this information to communicate with you, provide support, and improve our services.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Personal data is shared with prospective employers or candidates as part of our recruitment services. We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be disclosed if required by law or to protect our rights and interests.

Data Security

Protecting your personal information is our priority. We implement robust security measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

User Rights

Users have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of their personal data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, analyze site usage, and tailor our services. Users can control cookie settings through their browser preferences.

International Data Transfers

As a global platform, we comply with international data protection laws when transferring personal data across borders.

Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes.

Contact Information

If you have questions about our privacy practices or this policy, please contact us at utdaning@noroff.no
