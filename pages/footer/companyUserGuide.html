Get started with Noroff jobs

Registration

  • Click on the 'Registration' button located in the menu or navigation bar of the Noroff Job Agency Portal.
  • After clicking the 'Registration' button, select the 'Register as a Company' link.
  • Complete the registration form by entering your company details.This includes company name, contact information, and other relevant details.
  • Once you have filled out all the necessary fields, submit the form to complete your registration process.
  • After successfully registering, you are ready to begin exploring the platform, searching for talented students, and posting job advertisements.

Create job listings

  • Once logged in, navigate to the 'Create Job Listing' section. This can typically be found in the main menu or dashboard of your account.

  • In the job offer form, you will need to provide various details about the job position.These include:
    • Job Title/Position: Specify the title of the job or the position you are looking to fill. Location: Indicate the location where the job will take place. This could be a physical address for on-site work or specify if the position is remote.
    • Tags: Add relevant tags to help categorize the job listing and make it easier for students to find jobs that match their skills and interests.
    • Application Deadline: Specify the closing date for applications. This is crucial to manage the application process efficiently and to ensure timely responses to candidates.
    • Application Requirements: List any specific requirements or qualifications that applicants must meet. This may include educational qualifications, skills, experience, or other criteria.
    • Job Description: Write a detailed description of the job. This should include the responsibilities, required qualifications, desired skills, work hours, duration of the placement, and any other relevant information.
  • After filling out all the necessary information, review the details to ensure accuracy and completeness.
  • Submit the form to create your job listing.

  • Once submitted, your job listing will be visible to all students registered on the Noroff Placement Portal.
  • They will be able to view the details and apply as per the instructions provided in the listing.

Managing responses

  • To manage the responses to your job listings, visit your profile page on the Noroff Job Agency Portal.

  • On your profile page, navigate to the 'Applications' tab. Here, you will find an overview of all the applications received for your job listings.
    • For each application, you will see key details such as:
      • Position Applied For: The specific job title/position the candidate has applied to.
      • Candidate's Name:The name of the applicant.
      • Applied On Date:The date when the application was submitted.
      • Link to Application: A direct link to view the full application and any additional materials submitted by the candidate.
  • In addition to the applications, you can view the offers you have sent to candidates in the 'Offers Sent' tab.
    • Similar to the Applications tab, here you will find:
      • Position Offered: The job title/position for which the offer was made.
      • Candidate's Name: The name of the candidate to whom the offer was sent.
      • Offer Sent Date: The date when the offer was sent to the candidate.
      • Link to Offer Details: A link to view the details of the offer sent, including any terms and conditions, start dates, and other relevant information.

  • Review and Respond:
    • Use these tabs to review applications and offers. You can respond directly to candidates through the platform, facilitating a smooth recruitment process.
  • Keeping Track:
    • The portal's organization of applications and offers sent allows you to efficiently keep track of all your recruitment activities and ensure timely follow-ups with candidates.
